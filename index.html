Math formula flash cards

Hi friends,
Here is an interesting way for you to recap the math formulas we learnt in school last month.
Recollect the formula and hover over each flash card to check your answers!
After you are done with the square and rectangle, scroll down for cuboid and cube. Have fun!

Area of a square

Area = side × side

a = s2

Perimeter of a square

Perimeter = 4 × side

p = 4s

Area of a rectangle

Area = length × breadth

a = lb

Perimeter of a rectangle

Perimeter = 2 × (length + breadth)

p = 2(l + b)

Volume of a cuboid

Volume = length × breadth × height

v = lbh

Surface area of a cuboid

Surface area = 2 × ( (length × breadth) + (length × height) + (breadth × height)

s = 2 (lb + lh + bh)

Volume of a cube

Volume = edge length × edge length × edge length

v = e3

Surface area of a cube

Volume = 6 × edge length × edge length

v = 6e2
